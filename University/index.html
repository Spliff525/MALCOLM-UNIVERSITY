About Us

We are an accredited, private education provider that operates international colleges, English language centres across Africa, Europe, North America and an online learning platform to create life-enhancing experiences for students worldwide. Founded in 1991, we have been delivering international education students for 31 years. Today, our courses help over 50,000 Nigerian and international students achieve academic success every year. Students can join us for every stage of their academic journey.
